# Day Counter

This module shows the current Minecraft day as a short animated notification.

## Configuration Files

- `modules/announce/Days Counter/config.yml`
- `lang/lang_en.yml -> overrides.modules.day-counter.messages`
- `lang/lang_id.yml -> overrides.modules.day-counter.messages`
- `lang/lang_ch.yml -> overrides.modules.day-counter.messages`
- `lang/lang_jp.yml -> overrides.modules.day-counter.messages`

## Notes from source comments

- The source comment says the animation defaults to prioritizing actionbar output.
- Title and subtitle are available, but the default source leaves them disabled.
- MiniMessage formatting with `shadow` is supported in actionbar, title, and subtitle formats.
- The animation frames use `%day%` and `%world%` placeholders.

## `settings.debug-log`

Writes debug logs while the module runs.

## `settings.detect-current-day-on-join`

When `true`, the module shows the world day when a player joins.

## `settings.join-check-delay-ticks`

Delay after join before the day check runs.

## `settings.detect-on-world-change`

When `true`, changing worlds triggers a new world-day display.

## `settings.skip-after-antibot-verification`

When `true`, after anti-bot verification finishes, the player's world day is only recorded silently instead of being shown.

## `settings.world-change-delay-ticks`

Delay after a world change before the day animation is shown.

## `settings.world-whitelist`

If this list is not empty, Day Counter only runs in the worlds listed here.

## `settings.world-blacklist`

Worlds in this list are blocked from Day Counter.

Default source entries:

- `world_nether`
- `world_the_end`

## `settings.region-whitelist`

If this list is not empty, Day Counter only runs in the WorldGuard regions listed here.

## `settings.region-blacklist`

WorldGuard regions in this list are blocked from Day Counter.

## `settings.watch-online-day-change`

When `true`, online players are checked periodically for day changes.

## `settings.poll-interval-ticks`

Polling interval used while checking for day changes on online players.

## `settings.clear-title-after-animation`

Clears the title and subtitle after the animation finishes.

## `display.actionbar`

This section controls actionbar output.

- `enabled` turns actionbar on or off.
- `format` is the actionbar text format.
- `hold-refresh-ticks` re-sends the final frame during the hold period so it stays visible longer.

## `display.title`

This section controls title output.

- `enabled` turns title on or off.
- `format` is the title text format.
- `fade-in` is the fade-in duration.
- `fade-out` is the fade-out duration.

## `display.subtitle`

This section controls subtitle output.

- `enabled` turns subtitle on or off.
- `format` is the subtitle text format.

## `animation.step-delay-ticks`

Delay between animation frames. The source comment uses `4 ticks = 0.2s` as an example for smoother playback.

## `animation.fade-away-ticks`

How long the last frame stays visible before it is cleared.

## `animation.frames`

The list of animation frames shown in order.

Default source frames include:

- `--`
- `- D -`
- `- Da -`
- `- Day -`
- `- Day %day% -`
- `-- Day %day% --`

## `sounds.typing`

Sound used while the animation is still running.

- `enabled` turns the sound on or off.
- `name` is the sound name.
- `volume` is the sound volume.
- `pitch` is the sound pitch.

## `sounds.completed`

Sound used when the animation finishes.

- `enabled` turns the sound on or off.
- `name` is the sound name.
- `volume` is the sound volume.
- `pitch` is the sound pitch.
